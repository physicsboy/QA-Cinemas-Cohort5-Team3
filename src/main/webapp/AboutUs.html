Our Screens

Our screens are premium quality, with DTS 11.1 surround sound, and luxurious faux-goose down seats. Our premium seats are larger, with full armrests, upholstered with full leather.

Other than our seats, our cinemas are outfitted with a full confectionery stall and a Baskin RobinsTM ice cream stand.

We have a range of bundle deals for family trips or just a bunch of friends!
